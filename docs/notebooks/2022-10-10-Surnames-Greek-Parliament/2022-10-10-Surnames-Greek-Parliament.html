Structure of Dataset

Variable Property Description
Full Name qualitative
(nominal)		 Surname and name of the member of parliament
Party qualitative
(nominal)		 The party on which the MP got elected
Constituency qualitative
(nominal)		 MP got elected on this area
Term qualitative
(ordinal)		 TPlenum term

Thus, my sample has 4 variables, of which 7 are quantitative and 10 are quantitative properties, of which 8 are nominal and the rest ones (Education, Month) are ordinal.
